Reality One Group LOI

This unique and easy to use system allows you to structure and submit offers that fit your specific acquisition requirements. In just a few easy steps you can send a Letter of Intent (LOI) to the listing broker/agent to begin the purchase process. All offers are non-binding and only accessible by the listing broker/agent and owner.

The broker/agent may provide guidance in addition to the asking price such as a minimum earnest money deposit or maximum allowable closing period to help guide you toward what the seller is looking for. If no guidance is provided you are free to structure the offer with any Price, Earnest Money Deposit (EMD), Due Diligence Period or Closing Period desired.

Please note any information provided by users is transmitted using the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol, and stored on hardened servers with strict access control policies.
